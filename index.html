Key Insights

✈️

Aviation impact

One return flight London–New York emits roughly 1.7 tonnes CO₂ per passenger — equal to heating a UK home for 3 months.

🥩

Food choices matter

Shifting from a meat-heavy diet to plant-based can cut your food emissions by up to 73% — the single highest-impact lifestyle change.

🏭

Home energy

Switching to a renewable energy tariff typically cuts your home's carbon footprint by 1–2 tonnes CO₂ per year instantly.

🚗

Transport choices

An electric vehicle powered by an average grid produces ~3× less CO₂ than a petrol car over its lifetime, including manufacturing.
